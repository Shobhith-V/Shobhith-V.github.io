Autonomous Vehicle Systems Engineering

Engineering Project • April 2022 – April 2023

Robotics Autonomous Systems Embedded Systems
Code

As part of Team Abhiyaan, IIT Madras' autonomous ground vehicle team, I worked on the design and implementation of brake-by-wire and steer-by-wire systems for a commercial golf cart platform.

The system enabled full autonomous control and was evaluated at the Intelligent Ground Vehicle Competition (IGVC) at Oakland University, Michigan, where the team placed first among 25+ global universities in the Design Challenge. I also presented the team’s work to delegates from the education ministries of G20 countries, highlighting the system’s engineering and societal impact.

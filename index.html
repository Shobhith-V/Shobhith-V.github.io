01 — Who I Am

Engineer.
Researcher.
Builder.

I'm Lalanth Shobhith Vadlamudi, a final-year Dual Degree student at IIT Madras — B.Tech in Engineering Design and M.Tech in Data Science (graduating May 2026).

My research is driven by a deep curiosity about how biological systems process information and adapt in real time. I study the bidirectional interactions between brain and body, and how we can leverage these principles to build better neurotechnologies.

From modeling heart–brain dynamics with Neural ODEs, to deploying vibrotactile feedback systems at Cybathlon 2024 in Zurich, I pursue research that bridges theory and hardware.

04 — Featured Achievement
🏆 Global Competition · Zurich 2024

Team Asai at
Cybathlon 2024

Representing IIT Madras at the world's premier bionic Olympics in Zurich — the only Indian team in the Powered Leg Prosthesis Race. I designed the signal processing pipeline and closed-loop vibrotactile feedback system for real-time proprioceptive cues.

The system enabled transfemoral amputees to sense limb position through vibrotactile stimulation, improving balance and gait stability under competition conditions.
